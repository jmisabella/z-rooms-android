*All Hours Captured*


I can't hide from myself anymore.
A need to soothe. A morbid curiosity.
Now I'm exploring.
I cannot help myself.
I wake with demon thoughts,
chase a fleeting paradise again.

Driving to the city.
All hours captured.

Found out.
The cause of another's undoing.
Worry that devours
what little is left of her.

A plea expressed and ignored.
She asked me.
I heard her.
I went anyway.
