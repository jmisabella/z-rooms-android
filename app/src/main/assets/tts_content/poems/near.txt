*Near*


You are here when I am screaming,
here when I am still.
Here when the sky breaks open.
Here when nothing will.

Here when I am hollow,
not a speck, not a breath.
Here when we are laughing —
that wide, wild laugh you have,

the one that finds the quiet
and cracks it into light.
Those deep kind eyes that hold me
on every ordinary night.

The soul the world already loves
and answers when it calls.
And still — and still — you turn to me.
You chose me after all.


I will not say this is for you.
I'll set it on the shelf
and trust you'll find it there one day
and know it for yourself.
