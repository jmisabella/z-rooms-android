*The Known Shape*


The dream comes back
the way dreams do —
not announcing,
just already happening.

A tooth is in my palm.
I do not remember
the losing of it.
Only the small weight,
the clean root,
the fact of it
being where it should not be.

Another.
Then the hair,
more than a hand should hold,
and still coming.

In the dream
this is not alarming.
In the dream
a body is a thing
that gives parts back
and the giving is ordinary
and the rule is quiet.

I try to speak
and a tooth moves.
I try to speak
and another.

The mouth fills
with what used to be mine.

I wake in the dark
and do not move for a long time,
running my tongue
along the known shape
of a day
I have not yet been returned to.
